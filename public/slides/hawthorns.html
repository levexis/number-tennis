Computer Power Grows Really Fast


In 1965, future Intel co-founder Gordon E. Moore predicted that the number of transistors incorporated in a chip would approximately double every 24 months.

Almost 50 years ago he correctly predicted that this would lead to such wonders as

  1. home computers
  2. automatic controls for automobiles
  3. personal portable communications 


Storage space is increasing even faster, growing twice the size every 18 months



How tall would you be now if you had doubled in size every 18 months since you were born?
